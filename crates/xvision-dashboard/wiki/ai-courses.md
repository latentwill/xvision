# AI Courses

This page covers the course-market research for OpenClaw, Hermes, Vibecoding, and Claude Code.

## What the report says

### Landscape Report: AI Courses for OpenClaw, Hermes, Vibecoding, and Claude Code

- The market is crowded with low-differentiation "vibe coding" content.
- The gap is at the intersection of local agentic workflows, production debugging, and multi-tool orchestration.
- The strongest course angle is an integrated stack: OpenClaw + Hermes + Claude Code.
- Positioning should lean into practical, production-grade workflows rather than generic AI hype.

This is useful if xvision wants a course, funnel, or educational product around the toolchain rather than around trading alone.
