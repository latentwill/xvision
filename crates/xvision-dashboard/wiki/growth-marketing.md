# Growth / Marketing

This page groups the non-trading growth research from the 2026-05-29 drop.

## Included reports

### Promoting Low-Cost SaaS via Agents: Autonomous Marketing Methods and 60-Day Playbook

- Positions growth as an autonomous, low-overhead system.
- Prioritizes programmatic SEO, integrations, and community channels.
- Optimizes for a lean budget and minimal daily human oversight.

### Best Methods of Marketing to Architecture Firms: Automated GTM Playbook

- Defines the ICP for small and mid-sized architecture firms.
- Recommends highly targeted outreach that respects low-margin, risk-averse buyers.
- Centers measurable ROI and long sales-cycle realism.

### Meta Ads for Digital Agencies: Client Acquisition Playbook

- Treats Meta as a booked-call engine, not a generic lead-gen channel.
- Emphasizes specific, low-risk outcomes over vague "strategy" messaging.
- Focuses on high-value agency clients with a short payback period.

### Meta Ads for Info Products: Current Best Practices, Funnel Economics, and 90-Day Scaling Playbook

- Focuses on funnel architecture, CAC vs LTV, and measurement quality.
- Treats info-product advertising as an economics problem, not a creative gamble.
- Centers the 90-day launch and scale loop.

## Shared pattern

These reports all point to the same operating model: narrow ICP, clear economics, strong measurement, and repeatable channel execution.
