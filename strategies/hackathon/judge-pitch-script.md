# xvision — super-short presentation script

xvision is a regime-aware trading platform.

Instead of running one bot everywhere, it first identifies the market regime — trend, breakout, chop, bear, event shock, or risk-off — and then selects the strategy that fits.

In this pack, we show six behaviors: bull trend continuation, breakout confirmation, mean reversion in chop, bearish continuation, liquidation reversal, and a risk-off no-trade gate.

The key idea is simple: xvision can explain why it traded, why it skipped, and why that decision matched the market.
